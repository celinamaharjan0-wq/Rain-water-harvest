Welcome

Nepal receives abundant rainfall (1500–3000 mm annually), mostly during the monsoon season (June–September). Yet many regions — especially the mid-hills, Kathmandu Valley, and mountain areas — face acute water scarcity during the 8–9 month dry season.

Rainwater Harvesting (RWH) is a simple, low-cost, and proven solution widely promoted in Nepal by organizations such as SmartPaani, ICIMOD, NEWAH, and various local NGOs.

It helps families, schools, temples, and communities become more water-secure and resilient to climate change.
